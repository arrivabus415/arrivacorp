Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025 — How Arriva Corporation collects, stores and protects information.

Join our community
1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how Arriva Corporation handles information across all services, including RoAdmin, Licence Manager, RankGun, Arriva Connecting Roblox, Cermona Foundation, internal dashboards, Cloudflare Worker endpoints, Discord bots and any other connected tools or systems.
2. Information We Collect
Depending on the service, we may collect:

• Roblox User ID and username (for authentication or licensing)
• Discord User ID (for licence linking or verification)
• Licence keys, product entitlement data
• Server logs related to system errors or abuse detection
• Configuration or usage data for RoAdmin or internal tools
• API request metadata (timestamps, endpoints called, success/failure)
3. How We Use Information
Collected data is used strictly for operational purposes, including:

• Validating licences and permissions
• Operating backend systems and automations
• Securing infrastructure and detecting misuse
• Managing entitlements and product access
• Debugging system issues or API failures
• Enforcing usage limits and preventing fraud
4. Third-Party Platforms Used
Arriva Corporation relies on third-party services to operate core infrastructure:

• Roblox — identity, sessions, in-game access
• Discord — licence linking & verification
• Firebase — data storage for licensing systems
• Cloudflare Workers — API hosting, endpoints and routing
• BetterStack (optional) — uptime or failure monitoring

These services may have their own privacy policies, which users are also subject to.
5. Data Sharing
Arriva Corporation does not sell or trade personal data. Data may be shared only when necessary:

• To perform verification or entitlement checks
• To comply with legal requirements
• To prevent security threats or abuse
• To maintain operational functionality
6. Security & Protection
Systems implement layered security including rate limits, authentication checks, encrypted connections, abuse detection systems and access control.

While measures are taken to protect data, no service can guarantee absolute security.
7. Data Retention
Information is retained only as long as necessary for operational stability, legal purposes, system debugging or security enforcement.

Deprecated systems may retain minimal historical logs.
8. Arriva-Via AI (Deprecated Service)
Arriva-Via AI is no longer active or maintained.

• All user interactions with the historical AI system are considered legacy data.
• No active data collection occurs.
• No behavioural modelling, tracking or analytics are performed.
• Any stored logs follow the same retention rules as other deprecated services.

This clause exists to clarify legacy handling for a discontinued feature of the Arriva ecosystem.
9. User Rights
Depending on jurisdiction, users may request:

• Access to data stored about them
• Correction of inaccurate information
• Deletion of data where applicable
• Clarification of how systems process identity or entitlements

Requests must be made through official Arriva Corporation contact channels.
10. Children’s Data
Arriva Corporation does not intentionally collect additional personal data from minors beyond what is inherent to Roblox or Discord platform usage.
11. Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated. Continued use of Arriva Corporation services constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
12. Contact
For privacy-related questions, licence enquiries or data requests, please contact Arriva Corporation through our official communication channels.
