Core Features

Everything you need to manage players, handle reports, and maintain server quality.

Player Management

View all online players with real-time distance tracking, vehicle information, and speed monitoring. Select players via list or in-game click selection.

Moderation Actions

Kick, ban (with custom durations), teleport, spectate, and more. Every action includes reason tracking and detailed logging for accountability.

Report System

Players can submit reports with predefined reasons. Admins can claim, resolve, or dismiss reports with full status tracking and conflict prevention.

Spectate Mode

View from any player's perspective to investigate suspicious behavior. Toggle back to your camera instantly with one click.

Freecam System

Full 3D camera control with WASD movement, mouse look, and FOV adjustment. Perfect for investigating areas and monitoring player activity from any angle.

Announcements

Broadcast server-wide messages to all players. Includes cooldown protection and automatic formatting with your admin name.

Click Selection

Hold Left Ctrl and click on any player or vehicle to instantly select them. Works on all vehicle parts — automatically detects the driver.

Ban Management

Create bans with custom durations (hours, days, weeks, months). Unban players by username with built-in lookup functionality.

Action Logging

All moderation actions are automatically logged to Discord via webhooks. Track kicks, bans, teleports, and more with timestamps, reasons, and admin attribution.

Report System Workflow

A comprehensive player reporting system with status tracking and admin management.

For Players

Open Report Panel

Press C to open the report interface. You'll see all players within 1000 studs, automatically sorted by distance.

Select Player & Reason

Click the player you want to report from the list, then choose a violation reason from the predefined options.

Submit Report

Click Submit to send your report to online admins. Your report enters the admin queue for review.

For Admins

Access Reports

Open Admin Panel (Right Shift) and click the "Reports" button to view all submitted reports.

Claim & Investigate

Click a report to view details, then click "Claim" to mark it as yours. Other admins will see you're handling it.

Take Action

Use the main admin panel to spectate, teleport to, or moderate the reported player as needed.

Resolve or Dismiss

Mark the report as "Resolved" if action was taken, or "Dismissed" if invalid or duplicate.

Moderation Actions

Comprehensive tools for managing players and maintaining server order.

Kick

Remove a player from the server with a custom reason. Instant action with full logging.

Ban

Permanently ban with custom duration (hours/days/weeks/months). Includes reason tracking and unban functionality.

Teleport To

Instantly teleport to the selected player's location for investigation or assistance.

Bring

Bring the selected player to your current location for questioning or support.

Spectate

View from the player's perspective in real-time. Perfect for investigating behavior or monitoring gameplay.

TPR (Teleport Reset)

Teleport a player's vehicle to the nearest reset point. Useful for stuck vehicles or minor violations.

TPRK (TPR + Kick)

Reset vehicle to spawn point and kick the player. Combined action for vehicle-related violations.

Unban

Remove a ban by entering the player's username. Includes automatic UserID lookup functionality.

Announcements

Send server-wide messages to all players. Includes cooldown protection (30 seconds) and automatic formatting.

Clear Selection

Deselect current player and reset all form fields including reason and duration inputs.
